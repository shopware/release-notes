# Release notes Shopware 6.6.10.14

## Abstract

This is a maintenance patch for Shopware 6.6, includes ~ bug fixes

## System requirements

* tested on PHP 8.2 and 8.4
* tested on MySQL 8 and MariaDB 11

## Improvements

(No notable improvements in this patch release)
